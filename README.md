# Welcome to a-amusement network!

The social space that puts you first! - a-amusement is the next step towards a true, do-nothing social media, that's fully open source and yours to fork, selfhost and customise - and it's totally free, forever!

## Why is a-amusement so cool?!
a-amusement is, like, the coolest thing ever, since it's a social media site, digital pass system, a chat app, and overall, a cool place to hang out with your team! It's developed by me - [Thomas Hall](https://github.com/ralseimai) - with the help of Cursor, Figma and copious amounts of sleepless nights and swearing - the holy platform birthing trinity if you will!
